<p class="page-lede">MINNE Lab is a research group at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, developing computational methods for medical image analysis — mathematical modelling, image processing, and deep learning — with ongoing work in diffusion MRI and brain connectivity, clinical image analysis, and quantitative biomarkers for neurodegenerative disease.</p>
